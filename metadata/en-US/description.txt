meow is a native iOS port of the Android "meow" client — a full Mihomo proxy engine wrapped in a SwiftUI iOS 26 Liquid Glass UI, with traffic carried by a NetworkExtension packet tunnel provider.

If you have ever managed a Clash or Mihomo configuration, meow will feel familiar. Subscribe to a YAML config, choose a proxy group, flip the toggle, and your traffic flows through the proxy engine — all on-device, with no cloud component.

WHAT IT DOES
• Clash-protocol YAML subscriptions with auto-refresh and a built-in YAML editor
• Rule-based traffic routing (domain, IP, GeoIP, GeoSITE)
• DNS-over-HTTPS resolution
• Per-app proxy groups, latency tests, and selector switching
• On-device packet tunnel — no external server handles your traffic

SUPPORTED PROTOCOLS
Shadowsocks, Trojan, VLESS, WireGuard, TUIC, Hysteria2, and other protocols supported by the Mihomo core.

PRIVACY
Your subscription URLs, proxy credentials, configurations, and tunneled traffic stay on your device and are never transmitted by meow. The packet tunnel runs locally inside the Network Extension sandbox.

The app uses Firebase Analytics for anonymous, aggregate usage metrics (sessions, screen views, app/iOS version) to guide development priorities. Advertising identifiers (IDFA / AdID) are explicitly disabled, so no App Tracking Transparency prompt is shown. See the privacy policy for details.

REQUIREMENTS
• iOS 26 or later (iPhone and iPad)
• A proxy subscription you trust — meow does not provide any proxy servers

PUBLIC BETA
This release is a public beta. Some features are in active development. Please report issues on GitHub or via the in-app feedback link.
